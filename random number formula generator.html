Random Number Formula Generator

This page shows the forumula to use to get a range of random numbers based on a range you specify. Note that the formula displayed will create random numbers in the range you specified, inclusive of the end points.

The formula for computing random numbers is well-known and readily found on the internet with a simple search, but things get a little complicated if you want to create a range of random numbers tht does not begin with 0 or 1. For example, the standard formula found on the internet does not work very well if you want to produce, for example, a range of random number from 5 to 50. This tool will is able to adjust the standard formula to compute such ranges of random numbers, and will display the formula you need in your code to, for example, create a random numbers in a range from 50 to 100.

Enter the values for the inclusive range of random numbers you want to create. For example, if you want to create a range of random numbers from 1 to 6, inclusive, enter "1" as the low end of the range, and enter "6" as the high end of the range. If you want to create a range of random numbers from 0 to 60, enter 0 as the low end of the range, and enter 60 as the high end of the range.
